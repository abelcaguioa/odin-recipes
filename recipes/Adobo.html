Adobo

Description

This is a recipe post for Filipino Pork Adobo. It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Oyster Sauce

Steps in Cooking Adobo

  1. Saute garlic and pork
  2. Add enough water to make meat tender
  3. Add soy sauce and vinegar. Don't stir and let vinegar boil for 5 minutes
  4. Stir and simmer till meat is tender
  5. Add oyster sauce when pork is tender
  6. Serve with rice
